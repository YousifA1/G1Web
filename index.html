Unleash the
Hellcat

The Hellcat engine is a high-performance V8 engine produced by Chrysler. It has a displacement of 6.2 liters and is supercharged to produce a staggering 707 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque. The engine features forged pistons, a forged crankshaft, and a unique supercharger design to handle the extreme power output. It is primarily used in Dodge Charger and Challenger models, and is known for its impressive acceleration and top speed capabilities.

Dodge Charger
Hellcat Widebody

The Charger Hellcat Widebody is a variant of the Dodge Charger model with a wider body design for improved handling and stability. It features the same supercharged 6.2-liter V8 engine found in the standard Hellcat model, producing 707 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque. The wider body allows for wider tires and larger brakes to be installed, further improving performance capabilities. The exterior design also includes unique front and rear fascias, as well as wider fender flares for a more aggressive look.
